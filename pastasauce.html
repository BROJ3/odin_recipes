Spaghetti with tomato sauce

Description

The image below is not mine. As I make it again I will add my images. But nuff said, if done well this sauce can be delicious

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Stock
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Sugar
  • Spaghetti Barilla

Steps

  1. Dice up some onion and mince some garlic, while you are doing it set Olive il to be heated
  2. Fry Onion semi-gently on olive oil and add salt, don't let it burn
  3. When the onion is translucent, add garlic, reason is because garlic would burn before onion would be ready
  4. when they fry up a bit, add cherry tomatoes
  5. When liquid evaporates and onions and garlic go back to frying, add tomato paste
  6. when bottom is carpeting from tomato paste, add tomato sauce, basil and chicken stock or boullion
  7. keep cooking with lid on, on high heat
  8. Taste, if it's too bitter or acidic, add half a teaspoon to a teaspoon of sugar, stir and taste again. It does wonders.
  9. meanwhile add pasta, when pasta is halfway done, start putting some pasta water in sauce
  10. Few minutes before pasta is done, transfer it to the sauce and let it finish cooking in the sauce. This will allow it to soak up sauce
  11. Serve with original Parmesan cheese.
